Oberhauser Foundation

The Oberhauser Foundation manages the endowment for charitable giving. It follows a rather moderate and low-cost investment strategy with a 5% yearly payout rate to selected charities.

Aligned with the mission and the values of the Oberhauser Dynasty we are issuing yearly grants to the following causes. Each year the Council is re-eavaluating and approving the allocation.

5.00% Yearly Grants
50/50 Stock/Bond Split
< 1% Gross Expense Ratio

Yearly Capital Distribution

Grants are disseminated over the year to selected charities. The Remaining Capital - including contributions, capital gains, interests and dividends - is the endowment of the successive year.

About Us

Oberhauser Global is the online presence of the Oberhauser Dynasty. The mission is to educate members, bundle funds for charitable giving and align grants with the values and the mission of the Oberhauser Dynasty.

Charitable Giving for a Better Future

Charitable Giving is one of the main pillars of the Oberhauser Dynasty. We believe strongly in giving back to the community and on a bright future based on sustainable and positive social, scientific and technological progress.

Education as Foundation of Progress & Propersperity

Without learning from the past and transfering our knowledge to new generations we are bound to repeat our mistakes. Only with highly educated members we can make sure that our mission and vision is carried on across generations and with a positive footprint on the planet and humankind.

Services

Education

Knowledge and experience needs to be shared across generations. Not only from the older to the younger, but also vice versa. This cross-generational exchange assures that existing knowledge is preserved and new knowledge is created.

Charitable Giving

Stronger together. That slogan is true for families as well as communities and even for humankind as a whole. Charitable Giving from a pool of capital allows us to direct and align our support with our mission statement and our values.

Private Wealth Management

Sustainable private wealth management fuels the Charitable Giving over generations and assures that members have the right foundation to work towards a brighter future.
